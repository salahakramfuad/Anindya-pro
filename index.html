Frequently asked questions

Quick answers—full tools and guides are on the help center.

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days.
Can I return my bottle? +
Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy for unused bottles in original packaging. Custom color bottles are non-returnable. If the bottle is defective, record the unboxing so we can help with a replacement.
How does the self-cleaning work? +
UV-C light technology eliminates 99.9% of bacteria automatically every 4 hours when the bottle is stationary.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Is there a warranty? +
Yes! 6 months limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Self-cleaning components have 3 months warranty.
Does the bottle work with hot drinks? +
Yes! ThermoLock technology keeps hot drinks hot for 2+ hours and cold drinks cold for 6+ hours.
