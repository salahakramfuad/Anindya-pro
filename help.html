Frequently asked questions

Straight answers tied to how we actually sell and ship bottles today.

How long does shipping take? +
Orders usually leave our warehouse within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery is typically 3–5 business days after dispatch; express is often 1–2 business days. International times vary by country and customs—use the estimator below for a ballpark, and see your tracking email for the live date.
Can I return my bottle? +
Unused bottles in original packaging may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Custom color orders are final once production starts—check your confirmation email. If something arrives damaged or clearly defective, contact us with your order number and, if possible, photos or a short unboxing clip so we can replace it quickly.
What does “self-clean” or UV mean on your site? +
Special edition Arctic White includes a self-clean bundle in the box. On Ocean Blue, UV self-clean is an optional add-on at checkout (+$7)—not every bottle ships with UV hardware. Always read the bullet list on the product card so you know exactly what’s included.
What payment methods do you accept? +
Major cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay where available in your region. Final methods are shown at checkout.
What warranty do I get? +
Standard (750 ml, $9.99) bottles include a 6-month limited warranty against manufacturing defects. Special edition compact bottles list their own terms on the card—for example Rose Red notes a shorter warranty period. Keep your receipt or order confirmation for any claim.
Can I use it for hot drinks? +
The bottles are insulated stainless steel and suitable for typical hot beverages (coffee, tea) at normal drinking temperatures. Avoid overfilling, and let lids cool before opening. Do not microwave.
Membership vs one-time order? +
You can buy a bottle once from Products without a membership. Membership adds ongoing perks (reminders, deeper app features, family plans). You can add or skip a plan in the order form.
Do I need the app for every bottle? +
No. Classic Steel, Bright Black, and similar listings work fine as everyday bottles without pairing. Models that mention app reminders or smart tracking use the app for those extras.

Setup, care, and what’s realistic

Short guides based on how the lineup is described on the product page—no hype, just steps that help the bottle last.

📱

Apps, reminders, and tracking

If your card lists app reminders or smart hydration tracking:

  1. Charge fully before first pairing.
  2. Install HydroSmart from your app store and create an account.
  3. Add the bottle from Settings → Add device and follow the on-screen pairing flow.
  4. Set sip reminders to match your day—not everyone needs hourly buzzes.
🧼

Cleaning lids and seals

Bottles with a dishwasher-safe lid can go through the top rack; others should be hand-washed with warm, soapy water. Remove the seal weekly, rinse, and dry fully before reassembly to avoid odors.

☀️

UV add-on (select models)

If you purchased the UV option, run a cleaning cycle when the bottle has been sitting with water overnight or after a trip. It does not replace washing the lid and mouthpiece.

Pricing snapshot. Special editions start at $7.99 (500 ml compact). The main 750 ml lineup is $9.99, with optional UV on eligible colors. Full specs and side-by-side compare are on Products.
